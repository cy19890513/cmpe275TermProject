Top 10 Financial Losses Earthquake - This chart helps the user better understanding and estimating hazards from the earthquake. Preparing structures for earthquake is expensive and the level of investment is a social and political decision. With record and support, building codes can drafted to meet the demands of the expected shaking in a given region that are summarized by seismologists and earthquake engineer in hazards maps.

For the Top 10 Earthquake Losses chart, we use a bar chart to display it. In this chart, we arrange the earthquake that causes most financial losses in the first place and others from second place to tenth place based on the financial losses. It is easily and clearly for users to find the top 10 financial losing earthquake information. We use consistent colors throughout the chart. The labels are displayed horizontally in order to improve readability. And you can see the detailed information when you move your mouse on the chart.
