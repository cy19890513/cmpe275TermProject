Number of Earthquake in Record - Earthquakes are always occurring somewhere in the world. Millions of small earthquakes happen every year. This KPI shows the relative frequency of various magnitude earthquake with intensity 3.0 or above occurs in each country. 

For the Number of Earthquake in Record chart, we use region geochart to display it. In this chart, we use a single color from light to darker to present the number of earthquakes in recorded history. The darker color means the more number of earthquakes in history. It is easily and clearly for the user to find which countries are quake-prone. And you can see the detailed information when you move your mouse on the chart.
